Value-Added Services

Value-added services (VAS) refer to services that go beyond the core functionality of a product or service and add additional value to the customer. VAS can be provided by a variety of businesses and industries, such as telecommunications, finance, healthcare, and retail.

  • Marketing campaigns and Home Page : This function allows the carrier to analyze the history of the subscriber’s visit and his behavior on resources, display targeted advertising and information messages at any time, and segment subscribers according to the accumulated billing data.

  • Traffic monetization: The turnkey service for traffic monetizing is designed to generate additional profit for the operator by displaying advertising banners on HTTP pages.

  • Protection against DoS and DDoS attacks: Stingray Service Gateway has built-in protection against Denial of Service (DoS) attacks and Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attacks – these are types of attacks on computer systems when users cannot access the provided system resources or this access is difficult.
