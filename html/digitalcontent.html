Digital Content Intregation

An effective systems integration allows IT solutions to address business needs and mitigate transitions risk.

At Niwansolution, systems integration is about consolidating and customizing technology solutions to support the successful delivery of your most complex business challenges. Our global network provides a range of services—from advisory to implementation to system support—that integrate systems and technologies with a business-centric, value-driven approach.

Our systems integration services include:

  • Integration with packaged solution leaders, including Oracle, SAP, OpenText and Microsoft.

  • Advisory and implementation on large-scale business transformations.

  • Solution reviews and diagnosis, risk management and quality reviews.

  • Requirements gathering, custom development, system architecture and design.

  • Project and program management, quality assurance and solution delivery health checks.

  • Applying full-circle, IT-to-business thinking, our cross-industry teams help you limit cost and risk by streamlining integration with legacy systems and delivering testing, deployment and full project management solutions.
