Cyber Security

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computers, networks, and other digital systems from theft, damage, and unauthorized access. Cybersecurity is becoming increasingly important as more and more of our personal and business lives are conducted online. There are a number of different areas that cybersecurity covers, including:

  1. Information security: This involves protecting data and information from unauthorized access, theft, or damage. This can involve measures such as encryption, access controls, and data backups.
  2. Network security: This involves protecting computer networks from unauthorized access, malware, and other threats. This can involve measures such as firewalls, intrusion detection and prevention systems, and network segmentation.
  3. Application security:This involves protecting software applications from security threats such as hacking, viruses, and other forms of malware. This can involve measures such as vulnerability assessments, code reviews, and penetration testing.
  4. Operational security:This involves protecting physical assets such as servers and data centers, as well as ensuring that employees follow secure practices and policies.
